## PROMPT PER AL FONS DE L'ESCENARI (background)

```
Pixel art scene of a recreational motorboat in trouble in the English Channel on a summer day, wide horizontal view. Tense, urgent but not catastrophic atmosphere.

A white recreational motorboat (about 12 meters long) seen from slightly above and to the side, floating at a slight list (tilted a few degrees to one side). The boat has a small cabin with a windscreen, an open stern deck with bench seating, and a bow deck. Some items scattered on the deck from the impact: a cooler box tipped over, towels and bags displaced, a sun umbrella collapsed. The boat is clearly not sinking immediately but is in trouble — water is visibly lapping at one side of the hull lower than it should be.

The sea around the boat is the gray-green of the English Channel in summer — not stormy but choppy with small white-capped waves. No land visible nearby, but a faint gray-blue silhouette of coastline is visible on the distant horizon. A few seagulls in the sky. The weather is overcast but bright — high gray clouds with patches of blue, typical Channel summer weather.

Scattered on the deck: orange life vests (some on the seats, some on the floor), a first-aid kit open on the bench, a paper nautical chart unfolded on the cabin roof, a mobile phone on the dashboard, the boat's toolkit open with tools scattered around the engine hatch.
```
