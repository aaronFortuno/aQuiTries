Pixel art scene of a military air base with fighter jets, wide horizontal view. Tense, professional, high-stakes atmosphere.

In the center-left, a sleek fighter jet (single-seat, modern design resembling an F-18 or Eurofighter) parked on the apron with its canopy open, a boarding ladder leaning against the fuselage. No humans, no characters, no people visible anywhere in the scene. Empty airfield with jets and equipment only.

Color palette: tarmac gray, military gray-green camouflage on jets, yellow taxi lines, orange safety cones, olive green vehicles, pale blue sky, concrete gray hangar. The dominant feeling is professional military order — everything in its place.

Art style: pixel art aesthetic inspired by classic video game backgrounds. Visible blocky pixels, limited color palette (no gradients), clean hard edges, no anti-aliasing, no soft shading, no glow effects. Use 3–5 tones per color (light, mid, dark). The image should look like a high-resolution pixel art scene — large visible pixels as a stylistic choice, not a low-resolution image.

Lighting: bright harsh midday sunlight from above, casting short sharp shadows under the aircraft and equipment. The tarmac reflects heat and light. Strong contrast between the sunlit apron and the dark hangar interior.

Composition: wide horizontal format (landscape orientation, roughly 16:9), suitable for a game background, with visual space in the center or foreground where character sprites could be placed on top. The fighter jet with open canopy should be the clear focal point, creating a sense of readiness and urgency. The scene should feel like a stage or diorama.

Output: high-resolution image in landscape orientation. Full scene visible, no cropping.
