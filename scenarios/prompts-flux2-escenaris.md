# Prompts FLUX 2 — Escenaris de fons (sense personatges)

Cada prompt genera una imatge de fons en pixel art per a una situació del joc.
**Cap humà ni personatge visible** — només l'escenari i la situació.
Format horitzontal/panoràmic per servir de fons de pantalla del joc.

---

## Prompt maestro (base comuna)

```text
[SCENE DESCRIPTION HERE]

No humans, no characters, no people visible anywhere in the scene. Empty environment only.

Art style: pixel art aesthetic inspired by classic video game backgrounds. Visible blocky pixels, limited color palette (no gradients), clean hard edges, no anti-aliasing, no soft shading, no glow effects. Use 3–5 tones per color (light, mid, dark). The image should look like a high-resolution pixel art scene — large visible pixels as a stylistic choice, not a low-resolution image.

Lighting: consistent natural lighting appropriate to the scene, with clear but minimal highlights and shadows.

Composition: wide horizontal format (landscape orientation, roughly 16:9), suitable for a game background, with visual space in the center or sides where character sprites could be placed on top. The scene should feel like a stage or diorama.

Output: high-resolution image in landscape orientation. Full scene visible, no cropping.
```

## P1. La cacera del mamut — Campament de la tribu paleolítica

```text
Pixel art landscape of a Paleolithic hunter-gatherer camp in a temperate Eurasian setting, wide horizontal view.

A small clearing near a river bank, surrounded by sparse birch and pine trees. In the center, a campfire with visible embers and thin smoke rising, surrounded by a circle of stones. Around the fire, a few simple shelters made of wooden poles and animal hides, low to the ground, with fur bedding visible inside. Scattered around the camp: a drying rack with stretched animal skins, a pile of gathered firewood, a few stone tools and bones on the ground, a simple leather water-skin hanging from a branch.

In the background, rolling grassy hills fading into a cold gray-blue sky. The river is partially visible on one side, with flat stones along the bank. A distant silhouette of large animals (mammoths or bison) barely visible on the horizon, suggesting the hunt opportunity.

Season: late autumn or early winter. Muted, cold light. Bare branches on some trees, dry yellow grass.

No humans, no characters, no people visible anywhere in the scene. Empty environment only.

Color palette: natural cold tones — ochres, muted greens, grays, pale blues, warm orange from the fire embers, bone whites. No bright synthetic colors.

Art style: pixel art aesthetic inspired by classic video game backgrounds. Visible blocky pixels, limited color palette (no gradients), clean hard edges, no anti-aliasing, no soft shading, no glow effects. Use 3–5 tones per color (light, mid, dark). The image should look like a high-resolution pixel art scene — large visible pixels as a stylistic choice, not a low-resolution image.

Lighting: overcast cold daylight with warm glow only from the campfire embers. Consistent natural lighting with clear but minimal highlights and shadows.

Composition: wide horizontal format (landscape orientation, roughly 16:9), suitable for a game background, with visual space in the center where character sprites could be placed on top. The scene should feel like a stage or diorama.

Output: high-resolution image in landscape orientation. Full scene visible, no cropping.
```

## A2. L'arbre caigut — Carretera rural tallada per un arbre enorme

```text
Pixel art landscape of a rural mountain road in Catalonia blocked by a massive fallen tree, wide horizontal view.

A narrow two-lane asphalt road winding through a forested mountain area. A huge old oak or beech tree has fallen across the road, completely blocking passage. The trunk is thick and heavy, with broken branches scattered across the pavement. The root ball is partially visible, torn from the rain-soaked earth on the roadside, exposing dark soil and rocks. Smaller branches and leaves litter the road surface.

On one side of the road, a low stone wall typical of Catalan mountain roads, partially damaged by the fall. On the other side, a steep forested slope with pine and deciduous trees. The road curves away in both directions, disappearing into the forest. A small road sign (partly obscured by branches) and a weathered kilometer marker are visible.

Evidence of the recent storm: puddles of water on the road, dark wet pavement, a few other smaller branches fallen nearby, overcast gray sky with breaks of light suggesting the storm has just passed.

No humans, no characters, no people visible anywhere in the scene. No vehicles. Empty road only.

Color palette: wet greens, dark bark browns, gray asphalt, mossy stone wall tones, overcast sky grays with hints of pale light breaking through. Rain-darkened earth tones.

Art style: pixel art aesthetic inspired by classic video game backgrounds. Visible blocky pixels, limited color palette (no gradients), clean hard edges, no anti-aliasing, no soft shading, no glow effects. Use 3–5 tones per color (light, mid, dark). The image should look like a high-resolution pixel art scene — large visible pixels as a stylistic choice, not a low-resolution image.

Lighting: overcast post-storm daylight, wet surfaces reflecting subtle highlights. Consistent natural lighting with clear but minimal shadows.

Composition: wide horizontal format (landscape orientation, roughly 16:9), suitable for a game background, with visual space in the center or foreground where character sprites could be placed on top. The fallen tree should be the clear focal point. The scene should feel like a stage or diorama.

Output: high-resolution image in landscape orientation. Full scene visible, no cropping.
```
